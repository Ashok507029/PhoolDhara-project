Our Story

From Waste to Wonder

Every day, millions of flowers are offered in temples across India — and every day, those same flowers end up in rivers, streets, and landfills. We asked: What if they didn't?

Phooldhara was born from this simple idea — collect the flowers, preserve their fragrance, and return them to the devotees. Mandir ke phool, ghar tak khushboo — as pure handmade agarbatti and dhoopbatti.

1
Collect from TemplesWe partner with local mandirs to collect fresh floral waste every morning.
2
Sun-Dry & PurifyFlowers are naturally dried in sunlight — zero chemicals, zero additives.
3
Hand-Craft with LoveOur artisans hand-roll each stick with flower powder, herbs, and essential oils.
4
Delivered to Your DoorstepThe divine fragrance returns home — ready for your next pooja.
